## **Project Description**

**Predictive Intelligence Engine** is an AI-powered business forecasting and decision support platform that transforms historical and real-time business data into actionable insights.

The platform analyzes business datasets such as **sales, revenue, customer behavior, inventory, and market trends** using **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** and **Machine Learning (ML)** to forecast future trends and generate intelligent recommendations.

It features an interactive dashboard with dynamic charts, graphs, and key performance indicators (KPIs), enabling organizations to make faster, smarter, and data-driven decisions.

Built using **React.js, Tailwind CSS, FastAPI, and modern AI/ML technologies**, the platform provides a responsive, scalable, and user-friendly experience. By combining predictive analytics with explainable AI, it helps businesses optimize resources, minimize risks, improve operational efficiency, and enhance strategic planning across industries such as **Retail, Finance, and E-commerce**.
